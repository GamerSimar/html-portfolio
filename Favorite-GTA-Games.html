I prefer more story based GTA games. This includes GTA V, GTA IV, GTA San Andreas, and others. However, I will only talk about the top 3 GTA games listed.

I put GTA IV at 1 as it is widely regarded as the greatest GTA of all time. It has the best story, physics, characters. If GTA IV was remade it would sell more than GTA V in my opinion. GTA San Andreas has the best gameplay, a great story, the most customizable your player ever was. This includes clothes, tattos, and even body types like fat, skinny, or muscular. GTA San Andreas also arguably has the best GTA modding community too so it adds even content to the game. It also has the best 100% completion reward with a tank and jet spawning outiside of your main safehouse, infinite ammo, and $1 million. GTA V is the newest in the series and yet 10 years old. It has the best graphics, 3 playable characters, best cars, best online mode which still gets updated to this day. The only thing that seperates GTA V from GTA SA is the lack of rewards for completion, not too fun activities after story, basic customizations like clothing and haircuts only. These things defenitely put it below GTA SA.
