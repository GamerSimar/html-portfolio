How to mod older GTA Games

Modding GTA games is really easy. The first thing is getting modloader and cleo. Once installing these 2 mods, you can drop files folders created in the modloader which it will load. Some recomended mods are Silent Patch and Widescreen Fix.

How to beat the older GTA games

The older GTA games are pretty straight-forward to beat. However, there is one big exception, to beat GTA Vice City, you need to buy a lot of properties and need to buy the printer machine property. Then the rest of the game is pretty straight-forward, just complete all of the missions.

What to do after beating the GTA games

Most of the time, when you beat a GTA game, you 100% complete it. That is what I will tell you to do here.

looking up a guide is very easy and can tell you what to do in order to beat the game.

Check your game Percentage

Checking your game percentage can get you to know how much of the game you have completed. Checking your stats and cross referencing with the 100% checklist is also a good way to do so.
